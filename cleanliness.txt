Dataset: cleanliness.dat

Source: J.V. Fayard, A.K. Bassi, D.M. Bernstein, B.W. Roberts (2009).
"Is Cleanliness Next to Godliness? Dispelling Old Wives' Tales:
Failure to Replicate Zhong and Liljenquist (2006)," Journal of Articles
in Support of the Null Hypothesis, Vol. 6, #2.

Description: Experiment conducted where people were assigned to either
describe an unethical deed or ethical deed (treatment) and whether
afterwards they chose a gift of a cleaning wipe or a wooden pencil.
Also has simulated conscientousness scores to match mean and SD for
the wipe choosers and pencil choosers on each of 2 scales (CAC and CSC).
Univariate analyses only on conscientiousness scores.

Variables/Columns
Treatment   8   /* 1=Ethical   2=Unethical  */
Gift Choice  16   /* 1=Hand Wipe, 2=Wood Pencil  */
CAC Score   18-24
CSC Score   26-32
