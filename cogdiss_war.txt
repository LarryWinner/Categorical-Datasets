Dataset: cogdiss_war.dat

Source: A. Buckmaster and K. McKenzie (2009). "Cognitive Dissonance
and Students' Opinions on the Invasion and Occupation of Iraq,"
Defence Studies, Vol. 9, #1, pp. 118-128

Description: Results of 99 students responses to 2 questions on survey:
Q14: Do you agree with the US' occupaion of Iraq?
Q35: Was the US wrong to occupy Iraq?

Variables/Columns
Q14 Response  8  /* 1=Yes, 0=No  */
Q35: Response   16  /* 1=Yes, 0=No  */
