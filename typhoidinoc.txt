Dataset: typhoidinoc.dat

Source: G.D. Maynard (1909). "Statistical Study of Anti-Typhoid Innoculation,"
Biometrika, Vol. 6, #4, pp. 366-375.

Description: Results of innoculation versus no innoculation for typhoid
in 6 groups of soldiers.
Locations:
1=Stations Abroad
2=Indian Stations
3=17th Lancers
4=Coldstream Guards
5=Transvaal
6=7 Large Indian stations

Variables/Columns
Location    8
Innoculation  16  /* 1=Yes, 0=No  */
Typhoid   24   /* 1=Attacked, 0=Not attacked  */
Count 26-32
