Dataset:  remorse_death.csv

Source: T. Eisenberg, S.P. Garvey, M.T. Wells (2001). "Forecasting Life 
and Death: Juror Race, Religion, and Attitude Toward the Death Penalty,"
The Journal of Legal Studies, Vol. 30, pp. 277-311.

Description: Jurors view of defendant's remorse and first vote on
sentencing (both ordinal):
Remorse: Response to: "How well does 'sorry' describe the defendant?" 
1=Not at all, 2=Not so well, 3=Fairly well, 4=Very well
Jurors first vote:
1=Life, 2=Undecided, 3=Death

Variable names
remorse
jurVote
