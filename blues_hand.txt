Dataset: blues_hand.csv

Source: A.M. Cohen (1996). "The Hands of Blues Guitarists," American Music,
Vol. 14, #4, pp. 455-479.

Description: Location, Date of Birth, hand posture, and thumb style
for 93 blues guitarists born between 1874 and 1940.

Regions:   1=East, 2=Delta, 3=Texas
Years:     0=Pre-1/1/1906,   1=Post 1/1/1906
Hand Postures: 1= Extended, 2= Stacked, 3=Lutiform
Thumb Styles:  1=Alternating, 2=Utility, 3=Dead

Variable names
name   
state  
birthYr   (Year of Birth)
post1906    (1 if Post 1906, 0 if not) 
region       
handPost
thumbSty
