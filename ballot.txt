Dataset:   ballot.dat

Source: R. Darcy (1986). "Position Effects with Party Column Ballots",
The Western Political Quarterly, Vol. 39 #4, pp648-662.

Description: Study to determine whether ballot ordering is associated
with voting choice for presidential candidates.

Variables/Columns:

Order  5-8  /* DRL= Democrat/Republican/Liberterian  */
Party Choice  14-16  /* DEM, REP, LIB  */
Number of cases  22-24
