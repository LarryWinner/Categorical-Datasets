Dataset:  cohesion.dat

Source: T.A. Almy (1973), "Residential Location and Electoral Cohesion:
The Pattern of Urban Political Conflict", The American Political Science
Review, Vol. 67, #3, pp. 914-923

Description: Comparison of Electoral cohesion (high/low) for cities classified
by spatial residential location of social-class groups (segregated/integrated)
on 5 referenda:
1=Fluoridation
2=Education
3=Parks
4=Civic Improvements
5=Public works

Variables/Columns
Referendum   1
location type   4  /*  1=segregated  2=integrated   
Electoral cohesion within social classes    7   /*  1=low, 2=high
Numer of cities   10
