Dataset:  civwar1.dat

Source: C. Lee (1999), "Selective Assignment of Military Positions
in the Union Army: Implications for the Impact of the Civil War",
Social Science History, Vol. 23, #1, pp 67-97.

Description: Numbers of Soldiers by Rank (Private vs Higher) and Duty
(infantry vs Non-infantry) and Death Counts (Total, Illness, Injury)
by category for a sample of 4295 soldiers.

Variables/Columns

Private Indicator  1  /* 1=Private, 0=Higher Rank)
Infantry Indicator 3  /* 1=Infantry, 0=Noninfantry
Number of Soldiers  5-8
Mortalities (any cause)  10-12
Mortalities (illness)   14-16
Mortalities (Injury)   18-19
