Dataset:  deathqst.dat

Source: R.A. Kalish and D.K. Reynolds (1973). "Phenomenological
Reality and Post-Death Contact", Journal for the Scientific Study
of Religion, Vol. 12 #2, pp 209-221

Description: Responses to whether person has felt the presence
of anyone after the person had died, classified by ethnicity
for 434 LA residents.

Variables/Columns
Ethnicity   8  /* 1=Black, 2=Japanese, 3=Mexican, 4=White  */
Response   16  /* 1=Yes to feeling Presence   2=No  */
Count   22-24
