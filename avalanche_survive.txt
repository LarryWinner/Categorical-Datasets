Dataset: avalance_survive.csv

Source: H. Brugger, H.J. Etter, B. Zweifel, et al. (2007). "The Impact of
Avalance Rescue Devices on Survival," Resucitation, Vol. 75, pp. 476-483

Description: Survival of Avalanche by nation and amount buried.
Authors consider various other variables in analysis

Variable Names:
country   (1=Switzerland, 2=Austria)
buried  (1=Completely, 2=Partial/Not)
survived   (1=Yes, 0=No)
