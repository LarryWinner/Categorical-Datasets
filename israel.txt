Dataset:  israel.dat

Source: P. Burstein (1978). "Social Cleavages and Party Choice in
Israel: A Log-Linear Analysis", The American Political Science Review,
Vol. 72, #1, pp.96-109

Description: Cross-classification of Israeli citizens by: occupation
sector, type, and political party.

Variables/Columns:
Sector   8  /* 1=Private, 2=Histadrut, 3=Public  */
Job Type  16  /* 1=Manual, 2=Lower nonmanual, 3=Professional/Managerial */
Political Party  24 /* 1=Alignment, 2=Gahal, 3=NRP, 4=None */
Count  31-32
