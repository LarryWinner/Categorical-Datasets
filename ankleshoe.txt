Dataset:  ankleshoe.dat

Source: C.K. Curtis, K.G. Laudner, T.A. McLoda, S.T. McCaw (2008). "The Role
of Shoe Design in Ankle Sprain Rates Among Collegiate Basketball Players,"
Journal of Athletic Training, 43(3) pp. 230-233

Description: Incidence of ankle sprains among basketball players wearing
cushioned column and non-cushioned column shoes and wearing of ankle brace and
tape. Exposure indicates 1 athlete's participation in one game or practice.

Variables/Columns
Shoe Type   8  /* 1=Cushioned, 2=Non-cushioned */
Protection  16  /* 1=Braced, 2=Taped, 3=Both Braced and Taped, 4=Neither */
Ankle Sprain/* 1=Yes, 0=No  */
