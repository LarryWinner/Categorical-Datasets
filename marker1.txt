Dataset:   marker1.dat

Source: D.R. Shaffer, C. Sadowski (1975), "The Table is Mine: 
Respect for Marked Barroom Tables as a Function of Gender
of Spatial Marker and Desirability of Locale", Sociometry,
Vol. 38 #3, pp. 408-419

Description: Results of Field Experiment Involving
Marker Type (Male, Female, None) and Gender of 
Table Invader (Male, Female,None) in a college bar.

Variables/Columns
Marker Type   1  /* 1=Male, 2=Female,  3=None
Invader Type  3  /* 1=Male, 2=Female,  3=None
Count         5-6
