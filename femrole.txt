Dataset:  femrole.dat

Source: K. Kammeyer (1964). "The Feminine Role: An Analysis of Attitude 
Consistency", Journal of Marriage and the Family, Vol. 26, #3, pp.295-305.

Description: Women classified based on attitudes toward female personality
traits, Feminine Role Behavior, Numbers of friends, and dates.

Q's on Role Behavior Scale:

Q's on Personality Traits Scale:

women more sympathetic
women more moral
men better at reasoning logically
women more artistic
men more intellectual

Variables/Columns:
