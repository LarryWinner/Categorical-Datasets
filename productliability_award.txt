Dataset: productliability_award.csv

Source: R.L. Culp Jr., and D. Pollage (2002). "The Rhetoric of Strict 
Products Liability versus Negligence: An Empirical Analysis,"
New York University Law Review, Vol. 77, #4, pp. 874-961.

Description: Results of experiment with jurors being exposed to:
Judge's Instruction: Strict Liability/Negligence/None
Oral Arguments: Yes/No
All jurors hear facts of case.
Response: Award to plaintiff:  Yes/No
Jury Conditions:
1: Strict Liability/Oral Argument
2: Negligence/Oral Argument
3: No Instructions/No oral arguments (Control)
4: Strict Liability/No Oral Argument
5: Negligence/No Oral Argument

Variables:
Jury  (1-6)
juror (id - only used for ease of entering response)
strict liability   (1=Yes, 0=No)
negligence    (1=Yes, 0=No)
oralArg       (1=Yes, 0=No)
award         (1=Yes, 0=No)
