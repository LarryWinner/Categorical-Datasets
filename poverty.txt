Dataset:  poverty.dat

Source: Wenk and Hardesty (1993). "The Effects of Rural-to-Urban
Migration on the Poverty Status of Youth in the 1980s", Rural
Sociology, 58(1) pp. 76-92.

Description: Counts of youth classifed by poverty status, gender,
and race. 

Variables/Columns
Poverty 1   /* 0=No, 1=Yes  */
Gender  3  /* 0=Male, 1=Female  */
Race    5  /* 0=Black, 1=White  */
Count  7-9
