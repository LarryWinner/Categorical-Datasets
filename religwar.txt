Dataset:  religwar.dat

Source: S. Pearce (2005). "Religious Rage: A Quantitative Analysis
of the Intensity of Religious Conflicts," Terrorism and Political
Violence, 17:333-352.

Description: Classification of 278 territorial conflicts based
on religious/non-religious and whether religion has high/low relevance
(author explains how there can be non-religious/high relevance wars).
Response is intensity:
1= Low Intensity
2=Somewhat Low
3= Moderate
4=Somewhat High
5= High

Variables/Columns:
Intensity    8
Religious   16  /* 1=Religious, 2=Non-Religious  */
Relevance   24  /* 0=Low   1=High   */
# of conflicts   30-32
