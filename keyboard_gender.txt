Dataset: keyboard_gender.dat

Source: D. Rempel, A. Barr, D. Brafman, and E. Young (2007). 
"The Effect of Six Keyboard
Designs on Wrist and Forearm Postures," Applied Ergonomics Vol. 37, pp. 293-298.

Description: Stated Preferences among 6 keyboards (1=CONV,2=LAP,3=CURVE,
4=ELITE,5=ERGO,6=REVERGO) by male and female typists.


Variables/Columns
Gender   8   /* 1=F,2=M  */
Keyboard Preference    16
