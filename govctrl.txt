Dataset:  govctrl.dat

Source:  N. Dometrius (1979). "Measuring Gubernatorial Power",
The Journal of Politics, Vol.41 #2, pp589-610

Description: Cross-classification of administrators' views of
the level of governor's agency control by strength of their state's governor.

Variables/Columns:
Agency Control   1  /*  1=Legislature  2=About same  3=Governor  */
Governor's strength  3  /* 1=Very weak  2=weak  3=moderately strong 4=strong */
Count  5-7
