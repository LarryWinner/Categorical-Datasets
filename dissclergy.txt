Dataset:  dissclergy.dat

Source: J.A. Struzzo (1970). "Professionalism and the Resolution of Authority
Conflicts Among the Catholic Clergy," Sociological Analysis, Vol. 31, #2, pp.
92-106.

Description: Attributes related to differential responses to dissident clergy
in diocese wrt birth control.

Variables/Columns
Professional Autonomy Index  8  /* 1=Low Autonomy, 2=Medium, 3=High  */
Dissident type  16  /* 1=Public Dissidents, 2=Private Dissidents
                   3=Tolerant (to dissent) conformists 4=Intolerant
		   conformists*/
Count          23-24
