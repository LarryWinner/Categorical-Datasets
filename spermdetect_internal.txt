Dataset: spermdetect_internal.dat

Source: S. Sathirareuangchai, R. Phobtrakul, L. Phetsangharn, K. Srisopa,
S. Petchpunya (2015). "Comparative Study of Spermtozoa Detection Using the
Genital Swab versus Bedside Smear Slide Technique in Sexual Assault
Patients," Journal of Forensic and Legal Medicine, Vol. 35, pp. 69-72

Description:
Results of Internal tests for sperm for 2 methods of testing,
based on 724 subjects. For each test (on the same subjects), sperm
was classified as present or absent.

Variables/Columns
Genital Swab Test Result  8   /* 1=Present, 0=Absent  */
Slide Smear Test Result    16  /*   "          "      */
